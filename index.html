Morgana

Introduction

Morgana is a cat from the metaverse who is the second person that you unlock in your journey. He teaches the player mechanics of the game and is the only character who we don't see unleash a Persona. Morgana however, doesn't know who he is and why he is a cat.


Persona Unlocking

Morgana is the only character that we don't see unleashing his Persona as he started off with a Persona when we first met him held captive in the castle.
